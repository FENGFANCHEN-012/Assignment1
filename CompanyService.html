Blood Glucose Monitor

A blood glucose monitor is a portable device that allows individuals with diabetes to monitor their blood sugar levels anytime. By taking a small blood sample from a fingertip, the device provides accurate readings in seconds. Modern glucose meters also offer data storage and analysis features, helping patients manage their diet and treatment plan to control their blood sugar and prevent complications.

Electrocardiogram (ECG) Machine

An Electrocardiogram (ECG) machine is a vital tool for monitoring heart health. It detects the electrical activity of the heart by placing electrodes on the skin, helping doctors diagnose heart conditions such as arrhythmias, heart attacks, and other cardiac abnormalities. Modern ECG machines offer high precision and rapid analysis, making them an essential diagnostic tool in healthcare.

Ultrasound Diagnostic Machine

An ultrasound diagnostic machine uses high-frequency sound waves to non-invasively examine the internal structures of the body. It is widely used in pregnancy checks, organ scans, tumor detection, and more. Safe, with no radiation, it provides real-time images, offering doctors accurate diagnostic insights. Ultrasound has become a standard tool in hospitals worldwide.
